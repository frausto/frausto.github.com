The Developer

Hi I'm Nolan Frausto, your friendly neighborhood web developer.

I run Frausto LLC: a small single member company that specializes in building out custom web applications for clients.

The company was created in 2012 in order to provide programming and technical consulting services for websites in any stage of the development process. I follow Agile best practices, and believe in delivering quality code in a timely manner.

Primarily working on Ruby on Rails projects in the San Francisco area, I am a fullstack developer dedicated to delivering reliable, manageable, scalable, and well designed web products.

Working and living in the San Francisco area, I enjoy coding, climbing, museums, and good beer.
