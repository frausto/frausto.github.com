Building quality custom web products

Frausto LLC is a small single member company that specializes in building out custom web applications for clients.

Founded and operated by Nolan Frausto, the company was created to provide programming and technical consulting services for websites in any stage of the development process. As a consultancy, we follow Agile best practices, and believe in delivering quality code in a timely manner.

Primarily working on Ruby on Rails projects in the New York City area, we are dedicated to delivering reliable, managable, scalable, and well designed web products.
